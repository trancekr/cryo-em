Common Ice & Sample Problems
✓ Ideal
Even particles, thin vitreous ice
✗ Crystalline
Ice rings in FFT; slow cooling
✗ Aggregation
Clumped; instability or high conc.
✗ Concentration
Too few / too many particles
✗ Contamination
Ice crystals; humidity issues
✗ Thick / Thin
Poor contrast or empty holes
